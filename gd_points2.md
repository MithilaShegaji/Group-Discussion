## Education / Students

### Online education vs classroom education
1. Online offers flexibility; classroom offers structure and accountability.
2. Hybrid models combine accessibility with peer interaction.
3. Classroom gives better social learning and soft-skill practice.
4. Online scales expert teachers to remote areas.
5. Assessment integrity is stronger in physical settings.
6. Online needs self-discipline; not all learners thrive in it.
7. Classroom supports hands-on labs and real-time doubt clearing.
8. Digital divide makes online unequal in access.
9. Online content can be replayed, improving concept retention.
10. Best outcome depends on subject type and learner style.
